Founder's Message

Building Resilience Through Education

At Sadbhavana Sarvajanik Foundation, we believe that empowering farmers is the first step toward building resilient communities. When farmers thrive, their children gain access to education, and with education comes the promise of breaking cycles of hardship.

Education is the light that dispels darkness and opens doors of opportunity—especially for children born into hardship. Our journey is guided by a simple belief: when we educate a child, we empower an entire family and shape a stronger nation.

Dr. Khurshid Hussain Syed

Managing Trustee & Visionary
